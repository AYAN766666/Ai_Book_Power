# Chapter 04: Robot Kinematics and Dynamics

Welcome to Chapter 04 of "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics"! This chapter introduces the mathematical foundations for understanding robot motion: kinematics and dynamics. Kinematics describes the geometry of motion without considering the forces, while dynamics relates forces and torques to motion. These concepts are essential for designing, controlling, and simulating robotic systems.
