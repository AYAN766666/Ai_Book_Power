# Mini Quiz: Chapter 04

Test your knowledge of the concepts covered in Chapter 04: Robot Kinematics and Dynamics. Choose the best answer for each question.

---

1.  Which term describes the study of robot motion without considering the forces or masses involved?
    a) Dynamics
    b) Kinetics
    c) **Kinematics**
    d) Statics

2.  If you are given the joint angles of a robotic arm and need to find the position and orientation of its end-effector, which concept would you use?
    a) Inverse Kinematics
    b) Dynamics
    c) **Forward Kinematics**
    d) Trajectory Planning

3.  The Denavit-Hartenberg (DH) convention simplifies the representation of robot geometry by using how many parameters to define the relationship between adjacent links?
    a) Two
    b) Three
    c) **Four**
    d) Five

4.  A situation where a robot manipulator loses one or more degrees of freedom, making it unable to move its end-effector in certain directions, is known as a:
    a) Transformation
    b) Homogeneous Matrix
    c) **Singularity**
    d) Redundancy

5.  Which method for solving inverse kinematics provides a closed-form mathematical solution but is only feasible for robots with simpler kinematic structures?
    a) Numerical Solutions
    b) Geometric Solutions (iterative)
    c) **Analytical Solutions**
    d) Data-driven Approaches

---

**Answer Key:**
1.  c
2.  c
3.  c
4.  c
5.  c
