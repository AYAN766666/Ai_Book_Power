# Chapter 2: Key Concepts in Humanoid Robotics

This chapter delves into the core concepts of humanoid robotics. We will discuss the principles of embodiment, the importance of morphology, and the challenges of creating robots that mimic human form and function. Key topics include sensorimotor control, degrees of freedom, and the interplay between hardware and software in creating lifelike motion.
